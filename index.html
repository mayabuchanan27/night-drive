Pele: The Game

A story-driven adventure inspired by Hawaiian mythology, fire, land, and survival.

Game Pitch

Pele: The Game is an adventure game where the player explores a world shaped by lava, nature, and Hawaiian moʻolelo. The player must travel through different areas, solve challenges, avoid danger, and learn more about Pele’s power and connection to the land.

The goal of the game is not only to survive, but to understand the deeper story behind the environment, the characters, and the cultural meaning of each place.
