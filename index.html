NIGHT DRIVE

A story-driven adventure inspired by Hawaiian mythology, fire, land, and survival.

Story & Gameplay

Introduction

The game begins with the player driving home late at night on Oʻahu. The sky fades from orange into darkness as the road grows quieter. The radio hums softly while headlights cut through the empty highway.

As the player continues driving, something begins to feel off. The GPS signal fades. Road signs stop making sense. Without realizing it, the player ends up on the dark, winding road of Makapuʻu near Waimānalo.

Rounding a bend, a woman dressed in white suddenly appears in the headlights. The player is forced to stop.

She stands silently. Watching. Then slowly walks toward the passenger side.

The player must choose:

  • Let her into the car
  • Drive away

If the player lets her in, she enters without a word and sits in silence.

First Encounter: Menehune

Not long after, the car breaks down on a dark stretch of road in Waimānalo. The engine dies. Smoke rises from the hood.

The woman reveals herself as Pele and explains that small forest spirits are nearby and do not want her passing through.

The player must search for a missing engine part while defending against Menehune emerging from the forest. These spirits attempt to steal the part and delay the journey.

Once the player recovers the part and fends them off, the car is repaired and the journey continues.

Second Encounter: Night Marchers

The car breaks down again in a more isolated location. This time, the player must protect Pele while searching for parts, as Night Marchers appear in the distance.

The player must avoid direct confrontation, move carefully, and survive while completing the objective.

Final Encounter

In the final breakdown, the Menehune return, but this time they combine into a single, massive figure.

The player must defend Pele one last time while recovering the final part, facing the strongest version of the threat.

This final encounter tests everything the player has learned throughout the game.

Game Design and Illustrations

The visual design uses warm colors, lava-inspired backgrounds, island landscapes, and natural elements to match the theme of the game.
